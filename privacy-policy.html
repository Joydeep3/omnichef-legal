Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026
Notice: this document was drafted based on OmniChef's actual features, with reference to India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023, but has not been reviewed by a lawyer — have it checked before relying on it for App Store / Play Store submission or a compliance audit.

This Privacy Policy explains how "OmniChef" (a sole proprietorship owned and operated by Joydeep Mahato) ("we", "us", the "Data Fiduciary" under the DPDP Act) collects, uses, and protects your personal data ("you", the "Data Principal") when you use the OmniChef app and website (together, the "Service").

1. Who this applies to

OmniChef is a business tool for cloud kitchens and small food businesses in India. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you invite staff members to your business account, this policy applies to their use of the Service as well.

2. Consent

We rely on your consent as the legal basis for processing your personal data, as required under the DPDP Act. When you create an OmniChef account, you're shown a specific, itemized consent notice — separate from the Terms of Service — describing what we collect and why (see the table below), and you must actively opt in before your account is created. We don't pre-tick this for you.

  • You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account (Section 6) or by emailing us — withdrawal is as easy as giving consent was.
  • Withdrawing consent means we stop processing your data going forward. It doesn't undo processing that already happened while consent was in effect, and doesn't affect data we're required to keep for legal/tax reasons (Section 7).
  • Some processing (e.g. keeping GST-relevant financial records for the statutory retention period) is required by Indian law independent of consent, and continues even after you withdraw consent or delete your account, as described in Section 7.

4. Who we share it with

We use a small number of third-party services to run OmniChef. We don't sell your data to anyone.

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage (including your uploaded bill photos).
  • Google (Gemini) — processes bill photos and recipe links you submit, to extract structured data, under Google's API terms.
  • Razorpay — processes your subscription payment. Your card/UPI details go directly to Razorpay; we never receive or store them.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — delivers push notifications to the mobile app.

These providers may process data on servers outside India as part of their global infrastructure. We only share the minimum data needed for each provider to do its job.

5. How we protect your data

  • All data is scoped per business using database-level access rules (Row Level Security) — one business can never read another business's data, and staff members only see the business they've been invited to.
  • All traffic to and from the app is encrypted (HTTPS).
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • We never store your payment card details ourselves.

6. Data breach notification

If we become aware of a personal data breach that's likely to affect you, we'll notify the Data Protection Board of India and affected users as required under the DPDP Act, without undue delay. Where we notify you, we'll describe what happened, what data was involved, and what steps we're taking — by email to the address on your account, and/or an in-app notice.

7. Your rights

As a Data Principal under the DPDP Act, you can:

  • Access or export your business data (CA reports, recipe costs) at any time from within the app.
  • Correct or update inaccurate business/profile information yourself, in Settings.
  • Withdraw your consent to processing at any time (Section 2).
  • Delete your account and all associated data directly from Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account in the app. This is permanent and immediate, except where we're required to retain records (e.g., financial records) by Indian law. If you can't access the app, you can request the same by emailing us at joydeepmahato3@gmail.com.
  • Revoke a staff member's access at any time from the Staff screen.
  • Register a grievance about how we've handled your data — see Section 8 — and, if unsatisfied with our response, escalate it to the Data Protection Board of India.

We'll respond to a rights request within 30 days of receiving it.

9. Data retention

  • Your account and business data (inventory, recipes, sales, wastage, invoices) is retained for as long as your account stays active.
  • If you delete your account, we erase this data within 30 days, except for records we're legally required to retain — for example, invoice and GST-relevant financial records, which Indian tax law requires us to keep for a longer statutory period even after account deletion.
  • Bill/invoice photos and AI-extraction inputs are retained alongside the records they support, and deleted on the same schedule.
  • Device push tokens are deleted immediately when you disable notifications or delete your account.
