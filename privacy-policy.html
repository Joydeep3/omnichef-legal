Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026
Notice: this document was drafted based on OmniChef's actual features but has not been reviewed by a lawyer — have it checked against India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023, and any other applicable law before relying on it for App Store / Play Store submission.

This Privacy Policy explains how "OmniChef" (a sole proprietorship owned and operated by Joydeep Mahato) ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the OmniChef app and website (together, the "Service").

1. Who this applies to

OmniChef is a business tool for cloud kitchens and small food businesses in India. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you invite staff members to your business account, this policy applies to their use of the Service as well.

2. What we collect

CategoryExamplesWhy

3. Who we share it with

We use a small number of third-party services to run OmniChef. We don't sell your data to anyone.

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and file storage (including your uploaded bill photos).
  • Anthropic (Claude) — processes bill photos and recipe links you submit, to extract structured data. Anthropic does not use this data to train its models under our usage terms.
  • Razorpay — processes your subscription payment. Your card/UPI details go directly to Razorpay; we never receive or store them.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — delivers push notifications to the mobile app.

These providers may process data on servers outside India as part of their global infrastructure. We only share the minimum data needed for each provider to do its job.

4. How we protect your data

  • All data is scoped per business using database-level access rules (Row Level Security) — one business can never read another business's data, and staff members only see the business they've been invited to.
  • All traffic to and from the app is encrypted (HTTPS).
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • We never store your payment card details ourselves.

5. Your rights

You can:

  • Access or export your business data (CA reports, recipe costs) at any time from within the app.
  • Correct inaccurate business/profile information yourself, in Settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at joydeepmahato3@gmail.com. We'll delete your data within a reasonable time, except where we're required to retain records (e.g., financial records) by Indian law.
  • Revoke a staff member's access at any time from the Staff screen.

6. Data retention

We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal, tax, or accounting purposes.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. We'll update the "Last updated" date above; significant changes will be highlighted in the app.
