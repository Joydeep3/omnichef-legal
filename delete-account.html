Delete Your OmniChef Account

Last updated: 23 July 2026

You can permanently delete your OmniChef account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app — no need to contact support.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the OmniChef app and sign in.
  2. Tap the profile icon (top right of the Dashboard).
  3. Tap Business Settings.
  4. Scroll to the Danger Zone section at the bottom.
  5. Tap Delete Account and confirm twice when prompted.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your business profile, inventory records, recipes, sales and wastage history, scanned invoices, and your login credentials. This cannot be undone — there is no way to recover this data once deleted.

What isn't deleted

Any active Razorpay subscription is not automatically cancelled by account deletion — cancel it separately in Settings before deleting your account, or contact OmniChef.dev@gmail.com if you've already deleted your account and need help stopping billing.

No app installed?

If you no longer have the app installed but still want your account and data deleted, email OmniChef.dev@gmail.com from the email address your account was registered with, and we'll process the deletion on your behalf.
