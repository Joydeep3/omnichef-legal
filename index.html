Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Have queries about onboarding, features, or deployment? Find answers to the most common questions from kitchen owners.

How does the AI Invoice Scanner work?
OmniChef uses an advanced OCR model running on our backend. When you upload or take a picture of a bill (even hand-written local vendor bills or digital PDFs), our system parses the items, units, and quantities, and flags any GST/HSN details it spots on the bill for your review. It then attempts to auto-match items with your existing inventory. You review and click sync, and your inventory stock and prices are updated instantly.
Does the app support Indian GST rates?
Yes, OmniChef is fully GST-aware. It logs CGST, SGST, and IGST for every transaction. This lets you calculate true pre-tax raw material costs, account for your Input Tax Credit (ITC) correctly, and export a clean Chartered Accountant (CA) report at the end of the month.
What is the "YouTube Recipe Hook"?
Instead of manually keying in every ingredient for a new recipe, you can paste the YouTube cooking video link. OmniChef will scan the video's transcripts and descriptions to parse the raw text list of ingredients and sizes. It automatically converts vague units (like a pinch, a bowl, or cups) to standard grams and cross-references your live inventory ledger to give you a cost estimation instantly.
Can the app work without internet/WiFi?
Yes. Kitchen connection can be highly unstable. OmniChef keeps a local on-device cache and write queue for exactly this. You can browse recipes, check inventory, and log wastage or daily sales entirely offline. Once your WiFi reconnects, the app syncs everything back to Supabase automatically in the background.
Is there a limit on staff members?
No. Our pricing plans include unlimited staff members. You can invite your chefs, warehouse managers, or partners, and assign distinct roles (Owner, Staff) to control who has edit permissions or access to financial reports.
